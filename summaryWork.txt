S – Survey

The text discusses how artificial intelligence is changing healthcare, its benefits in diagnosis and patient care, and the ethical challenges it presents.

Q – Question

How does AI improve healthcare, and what concerns does its use raise?

R1 – Read

AI helps doctors analyze medical data quickly, detect diseases early, and improve decision-making. It also supports patients through chatbots and virtual assistants, making healthcare more accessible. However, issues like data privacy and loss of human interaction are concerns.

R2 – Recite

Artificial intelligence increases efficiency and accuracy in healthcare but raises ethical issues such as privacy and reduced human touch.

R3 – Review

AI has great potential to improve healthcare outcomes, but it must be used responsibly by balancing technological benefits with ethical considerations.
